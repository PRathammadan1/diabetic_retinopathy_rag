Severe Diabetic Retinopathy:

Description:
Many retinal blood vessels are blocked, reducing blood supply to retina.
High risk of progression to proliferative stage.

Symptoms:
- Noticeable vision problems
- Blurred or distorted vision
- Dark spots in vision

Causes:
- Advanced damage to retinal blood vessels
- Poorly controlled diabetes

Treatment:
- Intensive diabetes management
- Laser therapy to prevent further damage
- Regular ophthalmology visits
