Proliferative Diabetic Retinopathy (PDR):

Description:
Most advanced stage.
New abnormal blood vessels grow in the retina which can bleed.

Symptoms:
- Severe vision loss
- Floating spots or dark strings in vision
- Difficulty seeing

Causes:
- Long-term uncontrolled diabetes
- Extensive retinal blood vessel damage

Treatment:
- Laser photocoagulation
- Vitrectomy surgery in severe cases
- Strict diabetes control
