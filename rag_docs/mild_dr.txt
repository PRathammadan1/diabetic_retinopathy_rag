Mild Diabetic Retinopathy:

Description:
Earliest stage of diabetic retinopathy.
Small bulges in retinal blood vessels (microaneurysms) appear.

Symptoms:
- Usually no vision problems
- Occasionally blurred vision

Causes:
- Early diabetes-related blood vessel changes
- High blood sugar levels

Treatment:
- Control blood sugar, blood pressure, and cholesterol
- Regular eye exams
- Healthy diet and exercise
