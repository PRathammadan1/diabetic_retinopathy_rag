Moderate Diabetic Retinopathy:

Description:
Blood vessels that nourish the retina may become blocked.
Fluid or blood leakage can occur.

Symptoms:
- Mild vision problems
- Blurred vision
- Dark spots in vision

Causes:
- Diabetes affecting retinal blood vessels
- Prolonged high blood sugar

Treatment:
- Blood sugar management
- Regular monitoring by ophthalmologist
- Laser treatment if necessary
